# O‑CRA Framework

**Organisational Cognitive Resonance & Alignment**

A diagnostic model for measuring and improving AI integration at the organisational level. Built on the foundational CR&A (Cognitive Resonance & Alignment) framework.

## Quick overview
The O‑CRA framework defines six measurable dimensions:
- **Strategic Intent Alignment** – Does the AI act on your policies without being told?
- **Cultural & Linguistic Synchronisation** – Does it speak your team’s professional dialect?
- **Systemic Process Integration** – Does it support your natural workflow patterns?
- **Governance & Safety Architecture** – Do your AI policies empower instead of paralyse?
- **Institutional Knowledge Scaffolding** – Can the AI surface your organisation’s hard‑won knowledge?
- **ROI & Value Translation** – Are you tracking real business value, not just activity?

Each dimension has a published scoring protocol (see `/docs/ocra-technical-summary.md`).
